Who am I?

I am a lover of everything fun, quirky, and dogs. I am newly eighteen, and currently studying a bachelor of design, majoring in Visual Communication. Though I am a design student, painting will always be more fun. I just really need a job lol.
I am a very sentimental person, and try to find true value things and people who I believe that deserve it. To know someone in depth I will always hold dear.
I believe in equality through race, gender and all species, though insects can get fukd. And that despite knowing mankind has ruined the earth, there is potential in all of us to all take our part in restoring what was.
I am my thoughts, actions, and what I produce through my work. But pls do not critique me off this poorly executed website.


Why do I do design?

Design to me is something that I can do, create and make from picked up ideas or simple thoughts. It's a way of communicating through a visual image. Though some don't acknowledge the effort put into a design (those without a visual eye) it's still something that I'm willing to do because design can be so influencial and impacting without realisation. Design can promote the world in a perspective some have never seen, and that's my goal. To create something that makes people want to get up and make a change, through a visual image.
